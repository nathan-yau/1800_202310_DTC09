What is LifeLine?


LifeLine is a community-driven platform designed to connect people who live in the same neighborhood.

Our app aims to make it easy for neighbors to connect and share information under extreme weather conditions.

How do I log in?


Click on the log in/sign up button. If it's your first time here, enter your email address, your name, and then choose a password. Click save, and you're ready to go!

If you're a returning user, click on the log in/sign up button, enter in the email and password that you signed up with, and click sign in.

How do I find my community?


You can find your community by scrolling through the cards to view all available communities or by using the search bar.

Enter in your current location or postal code in the search bar and click the search button.

How do I post information?


Choose the community you wish to post to by clicking "Enter the Community". Once you're viewing that community, click on the teal button in the lower right corner of the screen.

In the pop-up window, give your post a title and what you want to share in the body section. You can also upload photos!
